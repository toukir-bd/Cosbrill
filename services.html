Our Services

Tax Services

We offer a wide range of tax solutions to help businesses comply with regulations and optimize their tax strategies.

  • Tax Consultation: One-time tax advisory, filing of tax returns (VAT, PAYE, SDL, WHT, Provisional Tax, Corporate Tax, Annual Returns).
  • Tax Training: Customized tax training for colleges, individuals, groups, corporate entities, microfinances, and SACCOS.
  • Representation Before TRA: Assisting clients with tax assessments, audits, appeals, and negotiations before the Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA)

Accounting Services

Our accounting solutions help businesses maintain financial accuracy and compliance.

  • Payroll Management: Payroll processing, payment facilitation, and statutory deductions (NSSF, WCF, etc.)
  • Asset Management: Asset valuation and preparation of Fixed Assets Registers.
  • Report Preparation: Financial and management reporting tailored to client need
  • Accounting Training: Training on financial statement preparation, financial management, and documentation best practices.

Business Advisory Services

We provide strategic business solutions to help clients optimize performance and drive growth.

  • Company Registration & Winding Up: Business name registration, company closures, updates, and BRELA annual returns.
  • Internal Controls & Policy Development: Development of financial policies, HR policies, risk management policies, and internal control systems.
  • Business Training: Training on business management, leadership, team building, branding, policy development, and internal controls.
  • Change Management: Support in implementing organizational change and ensuring successful adaptation.
  • Sales & Marketing Services: Strategy development to enhance brand awareness and revenue growth.
  • Organizational Design: Structuring organizations for optimal performance and strategic alignment.
